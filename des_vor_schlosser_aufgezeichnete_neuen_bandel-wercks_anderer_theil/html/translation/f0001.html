Of new bandworks, sketched from locksmiths, another part, each piece halved. By a locksmith in Nuremburg. Published by Johann Christoph Weigel. Klopffer. As some locksmiths beforehand paid heed that, in the first part of the band-works, not everything was meant to build and to work but only to deceive, so everyone is free, in this and the first part, to enlarge everything, to enhance, to omit much, and to adjust it according to their liking.
